Our Story

Founded in 2015, Windsor Residences began with a simple mission: to make finding the perfect home in Manila effortless and enjoyable. What started as a small team of real estate enthusiasts has grown into Manila's most trusted property platform.

Today, we help thousands of residents find their ideal homes, from cozy studios to premium penthouses, all equipped with modern amenities like pools, fitness centers, and smart living features.

Our Mission

To simplify property search through innovative technology while maintaining the human touch that makes finding a home special.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and comprehensive real estate platform in the Philippines, connecting people with their perfect homes.
